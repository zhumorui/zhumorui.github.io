Morui Zhu

1111 Budapest, Műegyetem rkp. 3.

Hi! I’m a Master’s student in Budapest University of Technology and Economics. My research interests broadly span computer vision, machine learning and computer graphics. In particular, I am interested in Radiance Fields and their applications in Virtual Reality (VR) and Autonomous Driving (AD). I am also excited about the creation of the next generation of media using Radiance Fields!

Outside of research, I am also concerned about the safety and reliability of AI. Besides, I am a devoted fan of Trustworthy AI. To this end, I registered the course: Artificial Intelligence and Law and attended the 1st Probabilistic Numerics Spring School. In addition, for the final project in the Computer Vision Systems course, I implemented a Gaussian Processes (GPs) model for few-shot image classification with uncertainty estimation.
